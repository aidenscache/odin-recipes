Burrito Recipe

Description

Quick, easy burrito/taco filling that is delicious and freezes great!

You could fill the tortillas and put them all in a baking dish and then cover with salsa and cheese and bake for about 10 minutes, if you don't want a mess at the table.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (4 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • Â¼ cup salsa
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Hot sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts and tomato sauce in a medium saucepan over medium high heat.
  2. To a boil add the salsa, seasoning, cumin, garlic and chili powder. Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. With a fork, start pulling the chicken meat apart into thin strings.
  4. Keep cooking pulled chicken meat and sauce, covered, for another 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Add hot sauce to taste and stir together (Note: You may need to add a bit of water if the mixture is cooked too high and gets too thick.)
